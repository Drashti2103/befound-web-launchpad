1. COMPANY IDENTITY & OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Company Name: beFound

Tagline: "Be Seen - Be Heard - Be Found"

Founded: 2025

Location: Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India

Company Story:
beFound was founded from a personal journey of transforming a passion for design
into a thriving business. What started as a modest freelance side hustle has
evolved into a full-service digital solutions company. The founder's journey
from freelancer to business owner reflects the company's core mission: helping
others be discovered through exceptional work.

Company Description:
beFound is a full-service digital solutions provider specializing in web
development, mobile applications, AI integrations, and custom software solutions.
We are a team of experienced developers, designers, and strategists who work
closely with clients to understand their unique challenges and create tailored
solutions that drive real results.

Positioning Statement:
"Innovative software solutions that empower businesses to reach their full
potential in the digital landscape. Our expertise transforms your vision into
reality with modern, scalable technology."
